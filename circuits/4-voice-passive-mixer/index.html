    4 Voice Passive Mixer

    This is one of the simplest circuits. It consists in a simple signal mixer with volume control for each voice.

    The resistors used can be lower than used in his schematics, as long as higher resistors cause signal loss. We chose here the 10k Ohm, but 1K would also fit, in order not to loose strength.
